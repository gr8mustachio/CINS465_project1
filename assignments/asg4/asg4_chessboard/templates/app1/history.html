The History of Chess can be followed all the way back to around 1500 years ago. While the origins themselves might be contested, we know for a fact that it originated in South Asia, more specifically India. It spread to the Middle East, then Russia and finally western europe by the 9th century. Chess as we know it today came to be around 1500 CE. There are six different types of chess pieces, and each side begins with 16 pieces The goal is to capture the other player's king, and both sides can capture as many pieces as the other side possess. Chess meta has evolved extensively as the years have gone on, with the current popular strategy being slow paced, claculated games where both players are (hopefully) thinking several steps ahead of the current move.
